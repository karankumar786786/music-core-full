# OneMelody S3 Storage Key Structure

This document outlines how files for Songs, Artists, and Playlists are structured in the S3 buckets (both the `onemelodytemp` bucket used during initial upload and the `onemelodyproduction` bucket where processed files are served).

---

## 1. Songs

Songs involve two separate upload flows: the audio file and its cover image. Both are linked together in the database, but they are stored under different S3 prefixes in production.

**Database Record:**
- `id`: `<songUuid>`
- `storageKey`: `songs/<jobId>` (This is the "audio" storage key prefix returned by the processing job)

### Temp Upload (Before Processing)
- **Audio:** `s3://onemelodytemp/<uuid>-<filename.mp3>` (Saved as `tempSongKey`)
- **Cover Image:** `s3://onemelodytemp/<uuid>-<filename.png>` (Saved as `tempSongImageKey`)

### Production Storage (After Processing)
- **Audio Files (HLS streaming):** 
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/songs/<jobId>/master.m3u8`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/songs/<jobId>/32k/...`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/songs/<jobId>/64k/...`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/songs/<jobId>/128k/...`
- **Cover Images:**
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/song-cover-images/<jobId>/cover/original.png`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/song-cover-images/<jobId>/cover/small.webp`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/song-cover-images/<jobId>/cover/medium.webp`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/song-cover-images/<jobId>/cover/large.webp`

> *Note for Frontend:* To display a song cover image, we take the DB `storageKey` (`songs/<jobId>`), change the prefix to `song-cover-images/`, and append `/cover/small.webp`.

---

## 2. Artists

Artists involve a cover image (avatar) and a banner image.

**Database Record:**
- `id`: `<artistUuid>`
- `storageKey`: `artists/<jobId>`

### Temp Upload (Before Processing)
- **Cover Image:** `s3://onemelodytemp/<uuid>-<filename.png>` (Saved as `tempCoverImageKey`)
- **Banner Image:** `s3://onemelodytemp/<uuid>-<filename.png>` (Saved as `tempBannerImageKey`)

### Production Storage (After Processing)
- **Cover Images:**
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/cover/original.png`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/cover/small.webp`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/cover/medium.webp`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/cover/large.webp`
- **Banner Images:**
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/banner/original.png`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/banner/small.webp`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/banner/medium.webp`
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/artists/<jobId>/banner/large.webp`

---

## 3. Playlists

Playlists follow the exact same structure as Artists, but under the `playlists/` prefix.

**Database Record:**
- `id`: `<playlistUuid>`
- `storageKey`: `playlists/<jobId>`

### Temp Upload (Before Processing)
- **Cover Image:** `s3://onemelodytemp/<uuid>-<filename.png>` (Saved as `tempCoverImageKey`)
- **Banner Image:** `s3://onemelodytemp/<uuid>-<filename.png>` (Saved as `tempBannerImageKey`)

### Production Storage (After Processing)
- **Cover Images:**
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/playlists/<jobId>/cover/small.webp` (etc.)
- **Banner Images:**
  - `s3://onemelodyproduction/playlists/<jobId>/banner/small.webp` (etc.)
